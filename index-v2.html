Avian Influenza Key Facts

Incubation Period: 3-5 days
Key Symptoms: Fever ≥38°C, cough, difficulty breathing
Transmission: Direct contact with infected birds
Confirmed Case: Positive RT-PCR test + clinical criteria + exposure history
