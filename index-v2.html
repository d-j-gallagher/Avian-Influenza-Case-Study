📋 Avian Influenza Fact Sheet ▼

Key Facts About Avian Influenza

What is it? Type A influenza viruses that typically infect birds but can also infect humans and other animals.

Transmission to Humans: Usually through direct contact with infected birds. Human-to-human spread is rare.

Incubation Period: Usually 3 to 5 days

Symptoms in Humans: Fever (≥38°C), cough, difficulty breathing, and potentially severe complications

Case Definition - Confirmed Case: Probable case (recent exposure + clinical criteria) AND positive confirmatory laboratory test (RT-PCR)

In Poultry: Highly pathogenic strains (HPAI) spread quickly and cause high mortality. Control involves depopulation (culling) of infected flocks.
