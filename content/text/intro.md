### Part A — Review of Influenza Data

The year is **2024**. On a Monday morning in early March, you started a new job as a surveillance officer focusing on influenza at the District D public health office, located in Province A in Country Z. Before you arrived, you reviewed your notes on surveillance.

An estimated **85,000** people live in District D. The district has one hospital (the General Hospital) and three health clinics: the District Clinic, the Children's Clinic, and the General Clinic.

Since influenza was a major focus of your work and you are particularly interested in zoonotic diseases, you decide to review the avian influenza fact sheet for Country Z.
