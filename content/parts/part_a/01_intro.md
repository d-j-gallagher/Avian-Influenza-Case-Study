# Part A — Review of Influenza Data

The year is 2024. On a Monday morning in early March, you started a new job as a surveillance officer focusing on influenza at the District D public health office, located in Province A in Country Z. Before you arrived, you reviewed your notes on surveillance.

An estimated 85,000 people live in District D, where you are assigned. The district has one hospital (the General Hospital) and three health clinics: the District Clinic, the Children's Clinic, and the General Clinic.

You are particularly interested in zoonotic diseases, so you decide to review the avian influenza fact sheet for Country Z.
