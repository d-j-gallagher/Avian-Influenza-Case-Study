# Context and figures

Country Z is a major poultry meat producer and has made efforts to improve avian influenza surveillance over time. Historically, Country Z has experienced outbreaks of avian influenza on poultry farms in several districts. Human cases have historically been rare, and caused mild self-limiting disease in poultry workers. However, because poultry farms are concentrated along migratory flyways for wild birds, there is ongoing concern about introduction/spread of new influenza viruses in poultry and people.
