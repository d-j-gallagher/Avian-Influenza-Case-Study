🦠 Avian Influenza Overview

Type: Influenza A virus that primarily infects birds
Incubation Period: Typically 3-5 days
Transmission to Humans: Direct contact with infected birds (rare)
Key Symptoms: Fever ≥38°C, cough, difficulty breathing
Severity: Can cause severe disease with high mortality in humans

🏥 District D Information

Total Population: 85,000 residents
Health Facilities:
• 1 Hospital: General Hospital
• 3 Clinics: District Clinic, Children's Clinic, General Clinic
Geographic Coverage: 8 towns

📊 Case Classification

Confirmed Case: Laboratory-confirmed (RT-PCR positive) plus clinical criteria and exposure history
Probable Case: Clinical symptoms and exposure history, but lab results pending or unknown

📐 Useful Formulas

Incidence Rate: (New cases / Population) × 100,000
Case-Fatality Rate: (Deaths / Total cases) × 100%
Proportion: (Part / Whole) × 100%
